This is a protocol-validation round on Stellar Testnet. Rewards are testnet XLM and have no monetary value. The submission, curation, and payment cycle is real; the money is not.

## Conditions

> There is no correct answer here, and no judge.
> If you have a text to leave, leave it; if not, you may simply pass through.

- Criticism may take any form, any length under 100 KiB, and any language.
- Disclosure of the publishable reading context in the manuscript frontmatter is required. See the participation guide.
- Not submitting is a legitimate response.
